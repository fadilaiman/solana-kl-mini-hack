# Research Notes

> Status: Planning. Placeholder for primary research artifacts.

This folder will collect raw research that informs the personas, journeys, and pain points:

- **Merchant interviews** — transcripts/summaries on willingness to accept stablecoin recurring billing and fee sensitivity.
- **Customer interviews** — reactions to one-time capped approval and trustless cancellation.
- **Competitive teardown** — how card billers (Stripe Billing), and existing crypto subscription attempts, handle recurring pulls.
- **Wallet UX observations** — friction points in connecting, approving, and revoking across major Solana wallets.
- **Survey data** — quantitative signals on adoption intent.

## Suggested file naming

```

```
